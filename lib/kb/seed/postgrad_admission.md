# Postgraduate Admission & Supervisor Matching — IPS

Source: UM Institute for Postgraduate Studies (IPS), Academic Services Department FAQ, FSKTM PG Handbook, UM Supervision Policy — `https://study.um.edu.my/`, `https://aasd.um.edu.my/`.

## Programme Modes

UM postgraduate programmes are offered in three modes:

1. **Coursework** — taught modules + project. No supervisor required at admission. Cohort-based intake.
2. **Research** — full thesis / dissertation. Supervisor required at admission.
3. **Mixed mode** — taught modules + research project. Supervisor required at admission.

## Pre-Application Supervisor Outreach (Research Mode Only)

For research and mixed-mode applicants, the strong recommendation is to contact prospective supervisors BEFORE submitting the formal application.

- Identify supervisors via faculty staff pages (e.g., `https://fsktm.um.edu.my/staff`).
- Email 1–3 prospective supervisors with: a research proposal (5–10 pages), CV, and degree transcripts.
- Without prior interest from a supervisor, research applications are usually rejected.

## Online Application

Submitted via `https://study.um.edu.my/`:

- Programme selection (specific to faculty).
- Field of study and 1–3 supervisor preferences.

## Required Documents

- Bachelor's degree transcripts (notarised + English-translated for non-English transcripts).
- Bachelor's degree certificate.
- IELTS / TOEFL / MUET (international applicants).
- Research proposal (research / mixed mode only).
- 2 academic referee letters.
- CV.
- Statement of purpose.
- Passport copy (international applicants).

## Application Review Sequence

1. **Faculty / department evaluation** — initial 5-working-day evaluation of completeness.
2. **Supervisor endorsement** (research/mixed) — prospective supervisor confirms willingness.
3. **Faculty Postgraduate Committee approval**.
4. **IPS final approval** (Senate-delegated).
5. **Offer letter** issued via `study.um.edu.my`.
6. **Acceptance & fee payment** — applicant accepts within deadline, pays first-semester fees.
7. **Registration in MAYA** — student status activated; supervisor assignment recorded.

## Supervision Policy

UM Supervision Policy mandates:
- Supervisor capacity check — limit per regulation; check current load.
- Expertise match between candidate's research area and supervisor's expertise.
- Lab / space / funding adequacy.

## Branching Conditions

- **No supervisor secured pre-application (research mode)** → application typically rejected; applicant routed to "expression of interest" pool.
- **Supervisor at capacity limit** → applicant offered alternative supervisor or co-supervision arrangement.
- **English proficiency not met** → conditional offer with English Intensive Course requirement before registration.
- **Bachelor's CGPA < 3.00** → may need APEL.A (Accreditation of Prior Experiential Learning) or industry experience pathway.
- **Coursework vs. research vs. mixed mode** → entirely different document checklist and supervisor flow.

## International Student Sub-Flow

Applicants from outside Malaysia trigger:
- **EMGS visa flow** before arrival (see EMGS Visa Renewal procedure).
- **Medical screening** at panel clinic.
- **Accommodation pre-booking** via UM Residential Colleges.

## Scholarship Pathways (Parallel)

- **MyBrainSc** — federal scholarship for Master's / PhD; separate application timeline.
- **Graduate Research Assistant Scheme** — UM-internal funded RA positions.
- **IPS Bright Sparks** — UM-internal scholarship for top admits.
- **JPA-PIDN** — for academic staff pursuing further studies.

## Candidature Confirmation

Master's by research and PhD candidates undergo a candidature defence (typically within first 1–2 semesters) to confirm continued enrolment.

## Common Pitfalls

- Students don't know they need to find a supervisor *before* applying for research mode.
- The list of "current openings" per supervisor is not centralised — applicants spam-email lecturers.
- Multi-sided wait state: applicant waits on supervisor, supervisor waits on faculty PG committee, faculty waits on IPS, IPS waits on Senate. No status tracker.
- Document re-uploads loop: small format issues (e.g., transcript not certified) reset the clock.
- International applicants juggle UM admission + EMGS + accommodation + scholarship in parallel with no consolidated deadline view.
