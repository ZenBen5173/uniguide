# Industrial Training Application — FSKTM WIA3001

Source: FSKTM Undergraduate Kit (UGKitS22425), FBE Industrial Training Guidelines, FSKTM Career Portal — `https://fsktm.um.edu.my/`, `https://fbe.um.edu.my/`, `http://careerportal.fsktm.um.edu.my/`.

## Eligibility

To be eligible for industrial training (WIA3001), the student must:

- Be in Year 3 Semester 2 (i.e., one full semester before the training period).
- Have completed all prerequisite credits for Year 1 and Year 2.
- Maintain a minimum CGPA of 3.30 (per FBE Industrial Training Guideline §3.1). Faculty-specific variation may permit a lower CGPA; faculties may set a CGPA floor as low as 2.50–3.00 with faculty appeal.
- Have no pending disciplinary action (clearance from HEPA required).

If the student's CGPA is below the FSKTM 3.30 floor, they may submit a faculty appeal letter to the Dean before proceeding with the application. Appeal does not guarantee approval; it is reviewed case by case.

## Pre-Industrial Training Workshop

- All students must attend the compulsory pre-IT workshop run by the Faculty in the semester before placement.
- Workshop covers: CV preparation, interview techniques, professional conduct, log book usage.
- Workshop attendance is enforced — non-attendance disqualifies the student from placement that intake.

## Company Sourcing

Two acceptable paths:

1. **Self-sourced** — student directly approaches a company. Company must subsequently be added to the SERU @ UM Centre of Records (CoR) approved-organisation list before placement is confirmed (1–2 weeks additional processing).
2. **Pre-approved** — student selects a company already on the SERU @ UM CoR list.

### Disallowed company arrangements

- **Family-owned companies** are explicitly prohibited (conflict of interest). This includes companies registered under a parent, sibling, spouse, or other immediate family member, regardless of whether the student has prior involvement with the company. Refer FBE Industrial Training Guideline §4.3.
- **Companies not in CoR-approved list** require new-organisation approval sub-flow (1–2 weeks).
- **Placement abroad** (outside Malaysia) requires separate funding application (Appendix 3 of the FBE guidelines) and is NOT available to international students returning to their home country.

## Online Placement Registration

- Student keys company details into the Industrial Training online application module (linked from MAYA / SPeCTRUM).
- If the company is not yet in the system, the Industrial Training Coordinator must create and submit it for CoR verification.

## Coordinator Approval (48-hour SLA)

The Industrial Training Coordinator must approve and confirm the placement through the online system **within 48 hours** of a successful match.

If approval has not been received within 48 hours, the student should chase via email; if no response within 5 working days, the matter may be escalated to the Industrial Training Committee chair.

## Confirmation of Industrial Training Placement Form

Form number: **UM-PT01-PK01-BR074-S00**.

The student must submit this form to the coordinator within **two (2) weeks** of reporting for duty at the placement company.

The form requires:
- Student details (name, matric no., faculty, programme).
- Company details (name, address, phone, supervisor, supervisor email).
- Placement period (start date, end date).
- Confirmation signature from the company supervisor.
- Confirmation signature from the student.

## Insurance Enrolment

Automatic via UM Group Family Takaful plan administered by HEPA. The student must be a registered student that semester (i.e., have registered WIA3001 in MAYA).

## Course Registration

Student must register WIA3001 in MAYA within the course registration window for the placement semester. This is **separate** from the placement approval — a student can have approved placement but unregistered course, which fails them out of the programme that intake.

## During Training

- Maintain the log book per faculty template.
- Faculty supervisor visits placement at least once during training.
- Industry supervisor evaluation submitted at end of training.

## Final Assessment

- Industrial Training Report (per FSKTM template).
- Presentation to faculty supervisor + moderator.
- Grading: industry supervisor (40%) + faculty supervisor (40%) + report/presentation (20%).

## Common Pitfalls

- Multiple parallel deadlines (workshop, placement approval, course registration, form submission, log book) with no unified view.
- The 48-hour coordinator approval window is invisible to students — they often don't know if they're "approved" or just "pending."
- Withdraw-then-reapply is required if a student gets a better offer — common gotcha.
- CGPA threshold is faculty-dependent and changes between cohorts; always check faculty-specific rules.
